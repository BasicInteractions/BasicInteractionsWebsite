Basic Interactions

More Real than Real

Our team at Basic Interactions is dedicated to developing haptic and force resistive controllers to interface with Augmented and Virtual Reality headsets allowing for more intuitive and responsive control. We are currently developing a glove that interfaces with an iPhone and provide both haptic feedback and physical restriction.

What We Do

Get to know Basic Interactions

Software Development

The Software Development team is currently developing physics and augmented reality simulations that run on a phone. In addition, we are developing code to handle the embedded system of the actuators inside the glove. We are a versatile team with an increasing knowledge of virtual and augmented reality.

Mechanical Design

The Mechanical Design team is currently prototyping the second version of the glove that has resistive and haptic feedback. We are transitioning from the first prototype with hard stop resistive feedback to the second prototype with increased tension to avoid only providing a hard stop resistance.

Passion

Basic Interactions comprises of a team of UIUC Engineering students who are passionate about technology and innovation. We are highly motivated and believe in the endless potential that our core technology will provide.

Business Development

The Business Development team is creating a business model to sucessfully bring our product to market. We plan to seek funding to accelerate the development of the glove and expand Basic Interactions from a passion to a business.
